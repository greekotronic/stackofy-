PQQ: The Mitochondrial Spark That Keeps You Young and Sharp

Alethia Research Institute · 8 min read · June 2026
TL;DR

PQQ is one of the few compounds shown to stimulate mitochondrial biogenesis - the creation of new mitochondria. Key points:

  • 10-20mg daily is the well-studied range - more is not necessarily better
  • Take in the morning with a fat-containing meal - it is fat-soluble and can affect sleep if taken late
  • Pairs exceptionally well with CoQ10 - one of the most synergistic combinations in mitochondrial health
  • Look for BioPQQ or PQQ disodium salt - the most studied and reliable forms
  • Not recommended for children or during pregnancy due to limited safety data

PQQ might sound like a typo, but this compound could be one of the most underrated tools in your longevity stack. Short for Pyrroloquinoline Quinone, it lacks the brand recognition of CoQ10 or NMN - but it punches well above its weight when it comes to cellular energy, brain health, and what scientists call mitochondrial biogenesis.

In plain language: PQQ helps you grow new mitochondria. That is a big deal. Most supplements aim to improve what is already there. PQQ helps you build more of the infrastructure itself - like upgrading from a flickering lightbulb to a freshly wired power grid.

The tree ring analogy is apt. Each ring represents a year of growth - new structure added to existing structure. PQQ does something similar at the cellular level: it signals the body to add new mitochondrial capacity rather than just optimizing what already exists.

What PQQ Actually Is

PQQ is a redox cofactor - a molecule that influences how cells produce energy and handle oxidative stress. It is found in trace amounts in certain plants, fermented foods, and even breast milk, but nowhere near the concentrations that appear to have therapeutic effect in humans.

Its primary mechanism of action involves stimulating PGC-1alpha, a master regulator of mitochondrial biogenesis. That is the molecular switch that tells your body to create more mitochondria - particularly in high-demand tissues like the heart and brain, where energy requirements are highest and mitochondrial decline with age is most consequential.

PQQ also scavenges free radicals directly in the mitochondria - where most cellular oxidative damage originates - protects existing mitochondria from damage, and helps modulate chronic inflammation. Three of the most important targets in anti-aging biology, addressed by a single molecule at therapeutic doses of just 10-20mg per day.

What the Research Shows

Animal studies are compelling. PQQ has been shown to increase mitochondrial number and efficiency, improve memory and learning, protect neurons from oxidative stress and toxin exposure, reduce damage in models of heart attack, and support liver metabolic function.

In humans, the research is still early but consistently positive. One study found that three weeks of PQQ supplementation led to improvements in energy levels, sleep quality, mood, and biomarkers of oxidative stress. Another showed enhanced cognitive performance and cerebral blood flow when PQQ was combined with CoQ10.

The CoQ10 Partnership

PQQ and CoQ10 are one of the most synergistic combinations in mitochondrial health. PQQ builds new mitochondrial infrastructure - CoQ10 fuels the energy production within it. Think of PQQ as the architect and CoQ10 as the power supply. Used together, they address mitochondrial health from complementary angles that neither can fully cover alone.

Forms and Quality

✓ Best Choice

BioPQQ®

The most studied branded form. Produced by Mitsubishi Gas Chemical via fermentation. Used in the majority of human clinical trials. Look for this name on the label for quality assurance.

✓ Recommended

PQQ Disodium Salt

The most commonly available form. Stable, well-absorbed, and used in most research. Ensure it comes from a manufacturer with third-party testing.

✓ Convenient

PQQ + CoQ10 Combo

Many quality supplements combine both in a single softgel. Convenient and cost-effective if you plan to take both - which you should.

Note

Generic PQQ

Quality varies significantly. If not using BioPQQ, verify the source and look for third-party COA confirming purity and form.

Children Not recommended - insufficient safety data

Take PQQ in the morning or early afternoon with a fat-containing meal. It is fat-soluble and absorbs poorly without dietary fat. Avoid taking it late in the day - it can increase mental alertness and energy in ways that interfere with sleep in sensitive individuals.
