Why 64% of NMN Supplements Contain Zero Active Ingredient

Most NMN supplements on Amazon are fake or heavily underdosed. Before buying anything, run through this checklist:

  • Certificate of Analysis from an ISO 17025 accredited independent lab
  • Purity stated as 98%+ beta-NMN specifically - confirmed by COA
  • GMP certified manufacturing facility
  • Clean, short ingredient list - no fillers or proprietary blends
  • Price between $40-80 per month at 500mg/day
  • Always add TMG alongside NMN - most people skip this and shouldn't

So if you tried NMN and felt nothing - there's a good chance the product was the problem, not your biology.

What NMN Actually Does

NMN is a precursor. Its whole job is to convert into something called NAD+ inside your cells. NAD+ is the fuel your cells run on - involved in energy production, DNA repair, and keeping your cellular machinery from falling apart.

The problem? Your NAD+ levels drop off a cliff as you age. By the time you hit 40, you've got roughly half the NAD+ you had at 20. By 60, even less. That decline shows up in ways you've probably already noticed - slower recovery, lower energy, brain fog, the feeling that your body just doesn't bounce back the way it used to.

That's where NMN comes in. You take NMN, your body converts it to NAD+, NAD+ levels go back up, and your cells start functioning more like they did a decade ago.

What NMN is not: a miracle cure, a substitute for sleep and exercise, or something you'll feel dramatically after day three. Anyone selling you that story is lying to your face. What it can realistically do over weeks and months with a quality product: support cellular energy, improve NAD+ levels measurably via blood tests, and potentially slow some of the cellular degradation that comes with aging.

The 90-Second Quality Check

Once you know what to look for, filtering out the garbage takes about 90 seconds per product. Here is what actually matters:

Before You Buy Any NMN Supplement

✓ Certificate of Analysis available? From an ISO 17025 accredited independent lab - not the company's own internal testing. If you can't find it within two clicks, walk away.
✓ Purity 98%+ for beta-NMN specifically? "High purity NMN" without a percentage means nothing. You need a specific number confirmed by the COA - and the COA and label should match.
✓ GMP certified facility? Look for "cGMP certified" or "FDA-registered facility." No GMP almost guarantees a questionable product.
✗ Wall of fillers or proprietary blends? Quality NMN needs nothing else. If you see a long ingredient list full of additives, you're paying for padding.
✓ Price $40-80 per month at 500mg/day? Quality NMN costs money to produce properly. Significantly below that - someone cut corners.

How to Dose It Correctly

Current human research points to a sweet spot of 250mg to 500mg per day for most people. Start at 250mg for the first two to four weeks, let your body adjust, then move to 500mg if you want to push further.

Take it in the morning. NAD+ is deeply connected to your circadian rhythm - taking it at night can interfere with sleep. Morning, with breakfast and a fat source. Not complicated but consistently ignored.

Give it at least 60 days with a quality product before making any judgments. The people who quit after two weeks because they "didn't feel anything" are the same people who plant seeds and dig them up after three days to check if they're growing.

The Stack Partner Nobody Talks About: TMG

When your body converts NMN to NAD+, it produces something called homocysteine as a byproduct. Elevated homocysteine is associated with cardiovascular issues - and it builds up silently.

TMG - Trimethylglycine, also called betaine - neutralizes that homocysteine through a process called methylation. It cleans up the mess that NAD+ production leaves behind.

Dose: 500-1000mg daily alongside your NMN. Inexpensive, widely available, and almost always overlooked. This is not optional if you're serious about NMN supplementation.

How to Measure Whether It's Working

You can feel great and have terrible biomarkers. You can feel nothing and have excellent ones. If you're serious about longevity, measure.

  • NAD+ blood test - companies like Jinfiniti offer at-home testing. Baseline before you start, retest at 90 days. Around $100. Worth every cent.
  • Homocysteine - confirms your TMG is doing its job. Ideal below 9 umol/L.
  • The 2pm test - the mid-afternoon energy crash is one of the earliest signs of improving NAD+ status. As levels improve, this wall often softens before you notice any other changes.
